Enchilada Sauce

  1. Heat a large saucepan, and saute the following ingredients untils soft:
    • Two tablespoons virgin oil
    • Large onion, chopped
  2. Sprinle in a quarter -cup o f flour.
  3. Jazz it up by adding.
    • Two tablespoons chili powder
    • Two teaspoons cumin
    • One teaspoon garlic powder
  4. Add a quart of water.
  5. Finally, add a teaspoon of salt, if desired.
Whisk as sauce thickens, then simmer for 20 minutes.
